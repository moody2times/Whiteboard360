Frequently Asked Questions

whiteboard animation

How is the cost calculated?

The length of your video determines the price. When determining the Length of your video, the word count of your script is the prominent factor we consider. This is a concrete way for us to calculate and is very accurate since we have refined the numbers over thousands of projects.

Will I Own full rights over the video?

Absolutely. Upon accepting delivery, you will have full rights to the video and can use it anywhere and everywhere you like. There are no additional fees or requirements to gain these rights. They are inherent.

Do you use templates for your videos?

ABSOLUTELY NOT! Each and every project is created from scratch for the sole use of our clients. This way, every project is guaranteed to be unique as the purpose it goes on to serve.

How much experience do you have?

We've created more than 10,000 videos just like these for clients in over 100 countries across the world. With more than 10 years of experience, rest assured, we are a team that knows how to get the job done right!

What video format should I expect?

Your video will be a high-quality MP4 file in 1080p HD. You can easily upload this file almost anywhere online because it compressed to use less data! Also, if you prefer mkv or other video formats, we've got you covered.

Can you provide a female voice over?

Yes! Whether you're looking for a female voice, a UK accent, Australian accent, or even a Spanish voiceover - we can provide that for you! We have several alternatives you can choose from.

Can you add my logo in the video?

Yes! You can incorporate any logo or graphic you like into your video. Just send us a high-quality JPG, PNG, or vector file to ensure the best possible result.

How long does the entire process take?

Standard turnaround time is 2-3 weeks. The first week is typically when we gather information, write your script (if you did not provide one), and prepare for your project. Then, once everything is approved, we begin recording the voiceover in week two and finish by creating the animations to match.
